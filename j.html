JASMIN BHASIN





Jasmin Bhasin is an Indian actress and model, known for her role of Twinkle Taneja in Zee TV's Tashan-e-Ishq.
Jasmin Bhasin also acted in a few South Indian films and had appeared in many commercial advertisements.
She made her film debut in the Tamil film Vaanam. She has also played the role of Teni in Dil Se Dil Tak on Colors TV.
She also appeared as a contestant in Khatron Ke Khiladi. She was last seen in Dil toh happy hai ji in which she portrayed the role of Happy.
If rumours are to be believed then she will also appear in Ekta Kapoor's Naagin 4.
